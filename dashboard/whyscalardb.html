🚑 Critical Care Coordination

Challenge: ICU patient needs immediate medication from pharmacy while updating vitals, insurance, and billing simultaneously across 5 different database systems.

❌ Traditional Approach

  • Pharmacy reservation might fail after vitals updated
  • Insurance verification could timeout mid-treatment
  • Manual coordination between 5 teams required
  • Patient safety compromised by data inconsistency
  • Average resolution time: 8-12 minutes

✅ ScalarDB Solution

  • Single atomic transaction across all 5 systems
  • Medication dispensed only if all conditions met
  • Automatic rollback if any system unavailable
  • Patient safety guaranteed by data consistency
  • Average resolution time: 200 milliseconds
🔬 Lab Results & Treatment Planning

Challenge: Lab results arrive and must trigger automatic updates to patient records, doctor notifications, insurance approvals, and treatment scheduling.

❌ Traditional Microservices

  • Lab service updates results in Lab DB
  • Async message to Patient service (might fail)
  • Another async call to Insurance service
  • Complex Saga pattern with 12+ compensation steps
  • Eventually consistent = temporarily incorrect data
  • Critical treatment decisions based on stale data

✅ ScalarDB Integration

  • Lab results trigger single distributed transaction
  • Patient, Insurance, Scheduling updated atomically
  • Doctor gets notification only after ALL systems consistent
  • Zero compensation logic required
  • Always consistent = always safe treatment decisions
  • Real-time analytics across all departments
💊 Prescription Management Across Providers

Challenge: Patient visits multiple specialists who prescribe medications. System must check for drug interactions, update insurance, verify pharmacy inventory, and maintain complete medication history.

❌ Fragmented Legacy Systems

  • Each specialist uses different EMR system
  • Drug interaction checks happen after prescription
  • Insurance approvals processed separately
  • Pharmacy inventory not real-time synchronized
  • Patient medication history incomplete
  • Dangerous drug interactions discovered too late

✅ ScalarDB Unified Platform

  • Single transaction across all EMR systems
  • Real-time drug interaction analysis before commit
  • Insurance pre-authorization included in transaction
  • Pharmacy inventory reserved atomically
  • Complete medication history always up-to-date
  • Patient safety assured by consistent data
